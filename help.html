Most frequently asked questions

Who can use this platform?
Only registered students (or verified users) can create listings, buy items, or contact sellers.
I forgot my password, what do I do?
Click “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the reset instructions sent to your email
How do I update my profile
Go to your account settings and edit your personal information.
How do I contact support?
Go to your account and click "Get help" next to your order.
How do I cancel an order?
You can cancel from your orders page before it ships.
How can I get a refund?
Request a return within 30 days from your account.
