How Campus Connect Works

Connect, Share Skills, and Earn Money on Campus!

Getting Started is Easy!

1. Sign Up

Create your free account using your student email. Choose to be a Service Seeker or Service Provider.

2. Complete Profile

Add your photo, student ID, and tell others about your skills or what services you need.

3. Find or Offer Services

Browse available services or list your own. Connect with students who need your help!

4. Connect & Earn

Chat, agree on terms, complete the service, and get paid. Build your reputation with ratings!

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can use Campus Connect?
Only registered students with a valid student email can use Campus Connect. This ensures a safe and trustworthy community.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. Enter your email, and you'll receive a reset link. Create a new password and log in.
How do I make a payment?
We support Mobile Money, Credit/Debit cards, Bank Transfer, and Cash on pickup. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
How do I list my service?
Click "Start Your Hustle" on the homepage. Fill in your service details, price, availability, and publish. Your service will be visible to all students!
How do ratings work?
After a service is completed, both parties can rate each other from 1-5 stars. Higher ratings help you get more clients!
How do I cancel an order?
You can cancel within 24 hours from your "My Orders" page. A full refund will be processed within 3-5 business days.
